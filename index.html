About Me


Hi, My name is Alex Coghlan, I’m 19 years old and I’m from a town in North Somerset called Weston-Super-Mare. My hobbies include football, gaming and social media. I am currently studying Internet Design at Plymouth University. So far, the course has been really interesting and I have enjoyed learning new skills and I’m looking forward to learning more skills in the near future.

This Portfolio will give you an inside into my personal social media pages. I enjoy using social media because I enjoy sharing my life with people and letting them know what I’m getting up to.

I take a lot of pride in my personal Instagram account. This is because I like photography and like editing and tweaking my photos. I like taking landscape photos and changing the colours and the effects of them. Whenever I’m abroad I take every opportunity to take photos of landscapes and other things and I like uploading them to my social medias so people will stay up to date with what I’m doing.
